How to Play

Goal: Collect all 3 keys and reach the exit before the monster catches you!

Controls: WASD or Arrow Keys to move, R to restart

Mechanics:

  • Keys: Collect all 3 to unlock the exit door
  • Monster: Chases you using your footprints - it learns your patterns!
  • Safe Zones: Green areas where monsters can't enter (brief safety)
  • Strategy: Plan your route, use safe zones wisely, and don't get cornered!
