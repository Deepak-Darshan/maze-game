How to Play

Goal: Collect all 3 keys and reach the exit door before the monster catches you!

Controls:

  • WASD or Arrow Keys - Move your character
  • Space - Place a bomb (if you have one)
  • R - Restart the game

Game Elements:

  • Keys (⭐): Collect all 3 glowing star keys to unlock the exit door
  • Exit Door: Opens when you collect all keys - reach it to win!
  • Monster: Red creature that chases you. It follows the shortest path and speeds up by 20% when on your trail!
  • Safe Zones (Green): Temporary safety from the monster. The monster can break them if you stay idle for 3+ seconds!
  • Checkpoints: Golden rings that save your progress. You'll respawn at the last checkpoint you crossed when you die
  • Spikes: Red tiles that damage you - avoid them!
  • Bomb Pickups: Collect bombs to break walls. Press Space to place, explodes after 1 second, breaks adjacent walls (8 tiles)
  • Speed Power-up (⚡): Blue circle that temporarily increases your movement speed

Strategy Tips:

  • Plan your route before moving - the monster learns your patterns!
  • Use safe zones wisely, but don't stay too long (monster can break them!)
  • Use bombs strategically to create new paths or escape routes
  • Activate checkpoints to avoid respawning at the start
  • Watch the monster's speed indicator - it increases when following your trail!
  • Don't get cornered - always have an escape route!
